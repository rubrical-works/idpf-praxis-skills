# Color Palette Reference
Material Design color palette for `.drawio.svg` diagram generation. Each color purpose defines a **fill** (light background) and **stroke** (dark border) pair.
## Semantic Colors
### Primary — Main Actions and Entities
| Role | Hex | Material Design |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| Fill | `#E3F2FD` | Blue 50 |
| Stroke | `#1976D2` | Blue 700 |
**Use for:** Default color for primary workflow steps, main use cases, core entities, standard/expected path. Most shapes should use Primary unless they need semantic distinction.
### Secondary — Supporting and Success
| Role | Hex | Material Design |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| Fill | `#E8F5E9` | Green 50 |
| Stroke | `#388E3C` | Green 700 |
**Use for:** Supporting actions, successful outcomes, secondary workflow branches.
### Warning — Optional and Deployment
| Role | Hex | Material Design |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| Fill | `#FFF3E0` | Orange 50 |
| Stroke | `#F57C00` | Orange 700 |
**Use for:** Optional steps, deployment actions, elements requiring caution.
### Danger — Errors and Destructive Actions
| Role | Hex | Material Design |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| Fill | `#FFEBEE` | Red 50 |
| Stroke | `#D32F2F` | Red 700 |
**Use for:** Error states, destructive operations, failure paths, abort conditions. Use sparingly.
### Info — Decisions and Configuration
| Role | Hex | Material Design |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| Fill | `#FFF9C4` | Yellow 100 |
| Stroke | `#F9A825` | Yellow 800 |
**Use for:** Decision diamonds (always), registry/configuration operations, branching logic.
### Neutral — Legends, Boundaries, and Disabled
| Role | Hex | Material Design |
|------|-----|-----------------|
| Fill | `#F5F5F5` | Grey 100 |
| Stroke | `#BDBDBD` | Grey 400 |
**Use for:** Legend boxes, system boundaries, disabled/inactive elements, structural shapes.

| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Fill | `#333333` | UML standard dark fill |

### Text
| Role | Hex | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|

| Subtitle text | `#666666` | Secondary labels, annotations |
